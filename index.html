Features
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.
📝

Natural task adding

Type like you text. "Buy groceries #shopping urgent" — BarfAI understands categories, priorities and dates automatically.

⏰

Smart reminders

Set reminders for specific times, multiple times at once, or daily for days. "At 9am, 2pm and 6pm" — three reminders, one message.

👥

Remind others

Send reminders to family and friends via WhatsApp. "Remind mom to take medicines at 8am" — they receive it on their WhatsApp.

🔁

Daily recurring reminders

Set habits and routines. "Daily at 7am for 30 days" — perfect for medicines, workouts, and daily habits.

😴

Snooze & dismiss

Not ready? Snooze for 15 min, 1 hour, 3 hours or till tomorrow morning. Mark done when complete.

📊

Daily summaries

Get a morning briefing and evening summary of your tasks. Know exactly what's pending and what you've achieved.

🌐

Hindi & English

Switch languages anytime. Send "hindi" or "english" — BarfAI responds in your preferred language from that point on.

📂

Categories & priorities

Organise with hashtags (#work, #home, #health) and priorities (urgent, high, low). Filter and focus on what matters.

🔍

Search & insights

Find any task instantly. Get smart insights about your productivity patterns — what you complete, what you snooze.

Privacy Policy &
Terms of Use

Privacy Policy

What we collect

  • Your WhatsApp phone number (account ID)
  • Tasks and reminders you create
  • Language preference

What we don't collect

  • Your name or personal identity
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Any financial information

How we use it

  • To send you reminders you've set
  • To store your tasks temporarily
  • To improve the bot's responses

Data storage
Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers (Asia region). We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.

Data deletion
Send "delete my account" to the bot at any time to permanently delete all your data. You can also email us at barfaicare@gmail.com.

Last updated: March 2026

Terms of Use

Acceptable use

  • Personal task and reminder management
  • Sending reminders to others with their consent
  • Using in Hindi or English

Prohibited

  • Using for illegal or harmful purposes
  • Attempting to hack or misuse the service
  • Harassment of any kind

Service availability
BarfAI is provided free of charge. We strive for 99% uptime but do not guarantee uninterrupted service.

Unsubscribe
Send "stop" or "unsubscribe" to the bot to stop all messages. Your data will be retained for 30 days then deleted.

Changes
We may update these terms. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
